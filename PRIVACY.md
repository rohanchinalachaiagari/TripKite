# TripKite Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

TripKite is an offline-first iOS travel companion for organizing trips, itinerary items, reminders, and travel documents.

## Information TripKite Stores

TripKite stores the information you choose to add in the app, such as:

- Trip names, destinations, and dates
- Itinerary items
- Locations and addresses
- Reminder preferences
- Notes and confirmation numbers
- Documents, images, and files you import into the app

This information is stored locally on your device.

## Data Collection

TripKite does not create accounts, collect personal information, track users, or sell user data.

TripKite does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Documents and Files

Any documents, screenshots, or files you add to TripKite remain stored locally on your device. TripKite does not upload these files to a server.

## Notifications

TripKite may use local notifications if you enable reminders. These notifications are scheduled on your device and are used only to remind you about itinerary items.

## Location Information

TripKite allows you to save location names and addresses for itinerary items. TripKite does not request access to your live location.

When you choose to open a saved location in Apple Maps, the location text may be passed to Apple Maps so it can search for or display that location.

## Children’s Privacy

TripKite is not directed toward children under 13. TripKite does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if TripKite’s features change. Updates will be posted on this page.
